# Vision

## One sentence

**Chasum is the AI-powered scheduling platform that lets service businesses run their entire booking operation — without duct-taping Calendly, spreadsheets, and SMS reminders together.**

## Problem

Small and mid-size appointment businesses (ultrasound studios, salons, clinics, consultants) lose revenue to:

- Double-bookings and manual calendar juggling
- No-shows without automated reminders
- Clients stuck on legacy tools (Picktime, phone-only booking)
- Staff time spent on admin instead of service delivery

## Solution

A single product that covers:

1. **Owner dashboard** — services, staff, calendar, clients, settings
2. **Public booking** — branded `/book/[slug]` flow with real availability
3. **Unified scheduling engine** — one source of truth for slots and conflicts
4. **Integrations** — calendar sync, email/SMS, webhooks, API
5. **AI workforce** (future) — agents that handle booking, reminders, and client comms

## First real customer

**GVM Baby World Ultrasound** is the design partner and dogfood tenant. Every feature must work for their day-to-day workflow before we sell it broadly.

## Long-term ambition

Become the operating system for appointment-based businesses: scheduling, payments, communications, analytics, and AI staff — multi-location and enterprise-ready.
