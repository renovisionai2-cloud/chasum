# Product Strategy

## Target customer (ICP)

**Primary:** Owner-operated service businesses with 1–10 staff and high appointment volume.

| Segment | Example | Why Chasum |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Design partner** | GVM Baby World Ultrasound | Complex service menu, emotional purchase, replace Picktime |
| Health & wellness | Clinics, therapists | Buffers, staff schedules, reminders |
| Beauty & personal care | Salons, spas | Multi-staff, service tiers |
| Professional services | Consultants, coaches | Simple booking + client CRM |

**Secondary (later):** Multi-location franchises, enterprise health systems.

## Positioning

| vs. | Chasum angle |
|-----|--------------|
| Calendly | Multi-staff, multi-service, business-owned brand, not meeting-links |
| Picktime | Modern UX, API, integrations, AI roadmap |
| Square Appointments | Independent brand, deeper scheduling engine |
| Custom spreadsheets | Reliability, automation, client self-service |

## Wedge

1. **Reliable scheduling engine** — unified slots, no double-booking (Phase 4 ✅)
2. **Replace incumbent fast** — migration path from Picktime (see [12_CUSTOMER_MIGRATION.md](./12_CUSTOMER_MIGRATION.md))
3. **AI differentiation** — natural-language booking and proactive workforce (Phase 5+)

## Go-to-market (current)

| Stage | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Now | Dogfood with GVM Baby World; prove one real business runs end-to-end |
| Next | Stripe billing, custom domain, embeddable widget |
| Later | Vertical templates (ultrasound, salon), partner referrals |

## Non-goals (for now)

- Generic project management
- Full POS / inventory
- HIPAA-certified hosting (evaluate per vertical later)
- Consumer marketplace (clients find businesses through Chasum)
