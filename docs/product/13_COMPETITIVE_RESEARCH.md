# Competitive Research

Living document. Last reviewed: July 2026.

## Landscape

| Product | Strengths | Weaknesses vs. Chasum |
|---------|-----------|------------------------|
| **Calendly** | Brand, simplicity, integrations | Weak multi-service/staff; meeting-centric |
| **Picktime** | SMB familiarity, affordable | Dated UX; limited API; GVM incumbent |
| **Acuity (Squarespace)** | Forms, payments | Squarespace lock-in |
| **Square Appointments** | POS integration | Less flexible scheduling engine |
| **Fresha** | Beauty vertical | Vertical lock-in, not ultrasound |
| **Mindbody** | Fitness/wellness scale | Expensive, complex |

## GVM Baby World context

Currently on **Picktime**. Pain points to validate in interviews:

- Reliability of availability display
- Mobile booking experience
- Reminder customization
- Service menu presentation (5 ultrasound types)
- Reporting / revenue visibility

## Chasum differentiation

1. **Unified scheduling RPC** — dashboard and public never diverge
2. **Modern stack** — fast, mobile-first, dark mode
3. **Developer platform** — API, webhooks (Phase 3 ✅)
4. **AI roadmap** — proactive workforce vs. static booking forms
5. **Owner-owned brand** — public page on your slug, not Calendly branding
